# USER MANUAL

## Homepage
- This is where they can see the opening/welcoming site of the system.
- There is a Button for Register as a new user or login with existing credentials.

### For Students
- Register/Login: Register if they dont have an account yet, Login if they have an account.
- View available schedules: They can view available schedules of different tutors and subject.
- Book tutoring sessions: They can book tutoring sessions depends on the schedule and wait for the admin approval.
- Leave reviews after completed sessions: After the tutoring session completed, they can review their tutor (1-5 stars).

### For Tutors
- Register/Login: Register if they dont have an account yet, Login if they have an account.
- Manage schedules:  They can create a new tutoring schedule for their subjects.
- View bookings: View scheduled tutoring sessions based on the admin's approval.
- Reviews: They can see feedback from their students.
- View Schedules: They can see their added schedules and the status of it.
